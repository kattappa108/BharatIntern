TEMPERATURE CONVERTOR

1. To convert temperatures in degrees Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 and multiply by .5556 (or 5/9).

2. To convert temperatures in degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply by 1.8 (or 9/5) and add 32.

3. To convert temperature in degrees Celsius to Kelvin, K= °C + 273.15

4. To convert temperature in degrees Farenheit to Kelvin, T(K) = [T(°F) + 459.67]× 5/9
